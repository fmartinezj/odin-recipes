It's An Egg, But Fancier! The Omelet.

The omelet is another classic breakfast item, and much like the pancake it is easy to make. An omelet typically contains cheese and veggies but anything can be tossed in to your heart's delight.

    Ingredients
  • eggs
  • cheese
  • tomato
  • onion
  • bell pepper
  • spinach
  • salt
  • black pepper
    Directions
  1. First, warm up a pan with a thin layer or butter or cooking oil. The add the veggies to the pan. Add salt and pepper as needed.
  2. Next, crack the eggs and add them into the pan. Toss the shells. Spread the egg out so it is evenly distrubted covering the pan and vegetables.
  3. Once the eggs are solid enough to move around, pour cheese onto the middle of the egg and begin to fold the sides onto itself covering the cheese.
  4. Flip the egg once for about 30 seconds and serve it with any desired toppings.
