Pancakes Done Simple

Ahhhh, the pancake. One of the most common breakfast items in the world, the pancake dates all the way back to the times of Ancient Greece. It is no wonder they have outlived empires because who can resist them! Even better, they are just as delicous as they are easy to make.

    Ingredients
  • milk
  • sugar
  • flour
  • eggs
  • butter
  • syrup
      • Directions
    1. First, mix your eggs, flour, milk and sugar together. Mix until the consistency is thick and thorough.
    2. Next, warm up the butter and mix it in with the pancake mix.
    3. Now pour the mix onto a hot and buttered pan, about 6 inches in diameter. Cook until bubbles pop then flip and cook until golden brown. Pour syrup and enjoy./li>
